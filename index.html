Who is Derek Dapp?

My name is Derek, and I like to code

Creating great, new things is one of my favorite parts of life. A never ending hunger for knowledge keeps me on the hunt for new things to learn. I found my start in programming by watching tutorial videos on YouTube. From there, I was introduced to Android Development.
